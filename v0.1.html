About v0.1
v0.1 — One hardcoded tour with GPS triggers. Goal: prove that maps + GPS + pins work together in a browser.
Three pins along a Fossvogsdalur walk. Walk into a pin's radius, get a chime and a note. Tap a visited pin to reread.
No authoring. No multi-device. No persistence. Those are v0.2 and beyond.
