Fieldnote · For guides

Your tour, live on every passenger's phone.

Fieldnote is a walking-tour companion for bus-tour guides. Passengers scan a QR code on the bus and get a live map: the stop you've activated, the pins worth seeing, where the bus is parked — and exactly when to be back.

Passengers never sign up and never install anything — they scan and follow along in the browser. It works on any phone.

What it does

One map. You decide what's on it, and when.

Live stops

Activate a stop when you arrive

Your tour is a sequence of stops. When the bus pulls in, you activate one — and the map wakes up on every phone at the same moment.

Pins per stop

Pins revealed only where you are

Each stop carries its own pins — viewpoints, paths, toilets, the café. Passengers see them only while that stop is live, so the map never drowns in the rest of the day.

Bus pin

Where's the bus? On the map.

Drop a pin where the bus is parked. The single most-asked question at any stop, answered on every phone before anyone asks it.

Departure timer

"Back at the bus by…"

Set the departure time when you open a stop. Every passenger sees the countdown. Running ahead or behind? Change it once — everyone's timer updates.

Passenger map

See your group as dots

Your guide view shows the group on the map — who's drifting toward the far viewpoint, who's already heading back. Round people up with your eyes, not a megaphone.

Getting access

Two ways in

Default

Create an account yourself

Open fieldnote.guide?role=guide and choose Create your guide account. Pick a name, your email, and a passkey — you sign in with name + passkey on any device. You'll get a recovery code at the end: save it somewhere safe.

Sign up as a guide
Beta

Have an invite code?

Early guides got a one-time invite code. Redeem it on the same screen — choose Have an invite code?, enter the code and your name, and you're in. The code also recovers your account on a new phone.

The routine

How a tour day works

  1. 1

    Create a session

    Morning of the tour: sign in, pick your tour, start a session for the day. A session is one bus, one day, one group.

  2. 2

    Share the QR on the bus

    Show the boarding QR from your phone (or a printout). Passengers scan it and they're on the map — no app store, no sign-up.

  3. 3

    Board

    Joined passengers appear in your session. Once the group is aboard, drive — the map stays quiet between stops.

  4. 4

    Activate stops as you go

    Pull in, activate the stop: its pins appear on every phone, the departure timer starts, and you drop the bus pin where you're parked. Watch the dots wander and come back. End the stop, drive on, repeat.

  5. 5

    End the day

    Back at base, end the session. Tomorrow's group gets a fresh QR — yesterday's link goes nowhere.
