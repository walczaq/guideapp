About v0.5
v0.5 — Stops + activation broadcasts. Tours have a stop → pin hierarchy. Guides activate stops; passengers see pins reveal as stops activate. Activated stops stay on for the rest of the session.
Chunks A + B + C done, plus session controls and a persistent arrival modal. Chunk A: stops + pins tables. Chunk B: guide-side activation. Chunk C: realtime fan-out to passengers. Session controls: deactivate a stop (tap Active pill), end the session (button at bottom of Control Center). Arrival modal: when the guide activates a stop, the passenger sees a centered modal with the stop name, an Icelandic huldufólk warning, and placeholder rows for time-at-stop, departure, weather, and guide notes. The modal stays up until the passenger taps "Got it" — and if they close the browser or lock the phone first, the modal re-shows with sound the next time they come back. Polling + visibilitychange backstop catches any realtime events that go missing. Future: Web Push for closed-app passengers (D), iOS-foreground lobby copy (E).
v0.4 still works: invite-code auth, live guide view, realtime passenger dots, breadcrumb trails. v0.3 still works: GPS fixes captured locally, queued in IndexedDB, synced to Supabase when network is available.

Welcome to Fieldnote

Your live tour companion

Fieldnote shows you on the tour map and reveals each stop as your guide activates it — plus warnings and "time to head back to the bus" alerts.

📍 Why location? So you appear on the map and stops light up near you. It's only shared during this tour — never in the background.
📲 Adding to your home screen is safe. It only runs while you're using it or during the tour — it can't track you in the background — and you can delete it in one tap, like any app.
Step 1 of 3

Stay notified

Get a buzz when your guide starts a stop, sends a warning, calls everyone back to the bus, or changes the departure time — even with your phone locked.

Step 2 of 3

Share your location

Allow location so you show up on the map and stops reveal as you reach them. Only shared with your guide during this tour.

Step 3 of 3

Add to home screen

Install Fieldnote for reliable notifications even when the app is closed. Safe to add, easy to remove.

You're set

When notifications reach you

Android: work even with the app closed once notifications are on.
iPhone: work when you've added Fieldnote to your home screen. In a plain Safari tab, keep it open during the tour.
If your phone aggressively closes apps, keep Fieldnote open during key moments to be safe.
